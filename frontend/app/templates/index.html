Hello, traveler!

Welcome to our EV Charging Station Information Hub!

Our software provides quick access to essential data regarding electric vehicle charging stations in Milan.
Here's what you can do in each section:

1.  Number of charging stations:
By providing the name of the street you are on, you will get the number of charging stations currently present in that street.

2.  Providers:
You can select from a drop down menu your charging stations' provider, to get the location of all their charging stations, their type, and the number of charging points in each location.

3.  Socket type:
By providing the name of the street you are on and the house number, you will get the type of charging station you're looking at.

4.  Streets:
You can select from a drop down menu the name of the area you need, and you'll get the location of all charging stations currently present in that area.

5.  Your provider given street name:
By providing the name of the street you are on, you will get the provider for the charging stations on that street.

Our software aims to simplify your EV charging station search by providing precise and detailed information, ensuring a seamless experience for electric vehicle owners in Milan.
